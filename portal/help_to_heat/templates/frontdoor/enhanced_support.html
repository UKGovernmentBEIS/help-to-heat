You may qualify for enhanced household support from your local council

Your local council may be able to offer you more help than you can receive through this service.

Because your household income is less than £31,000 and no one in your household receives benefits, your local council may be able to offer multiple home energy saving measures for your property. This is known as the ‘Energy Company Obligation 4 – Flexible Entry Route’.

Only some local councils are able to offer this. Find your council contact details to contact them directly (opens in a new tab).

You can still use this service to check what other home energy schemes you may qualify for.
