Do you have a loft that hasn’t been converted into a room?

Help me answer this question

A loft is an unheated space without walls and ceilings, immediately beneath the pitched roof. You will usually be able to see the roof timbers.

If all the space under your pitched roof, or nearly all the space, is used as living space with finished walls and ceilings then the loft has been converted into a room.

Homes of your type typically do not have a loft.
